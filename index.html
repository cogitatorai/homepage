You've told your AI the same thing three times this week. Your partner told it something useful yesterday, but your AI has no idea. Everyone in the house talks to their own separate assistant, and none of them learn from each other.

Cogitator remembers. And it shares what it learns.

It knows you

One person tells the agent about a family trip, and everyone's assistant knows about it. A teammate documents a decision, and the whole team benefits. The more people use it, the richer the knowledge graph becomes.

Facts connect to preferences. Preferences connect to patterns. Each person adds their own piece, and the agent builds a picture that none of them could have built alone.

Built-in Privacy

Each person controls what stays private and what gets shared. Personal conversations are personal. Shared knowledge benefits the group. You decide the boundary.

It works while you sleep

Wake up to a briefing pulled from your calendar, email, and the weather. Get notified when a website you're watching changes. Weekly reports, written and waiting.

Schedule anything the agent can do in conversation. It runs on its own, with the same tools and the same memory.

When something breaks, it fixes itself

The agent reads the failure log, figures out what went wrong, and rewrites the task. Transient problems get retried. Structural problems get fixed. It never works around security boundaries to force a pass.

"On day one, it's an assistant.
By day thirty, it's yours."

It grows with you

Every conversation teaches it something new. Every tool you connect makes it more capable. Your Cogitator on day 100 is a different agent than on day 1.

Connect your calendar, your email, your tools. It figures out the rest. It writes its own skills, so what it learns once, it remembers how to do next time.

Bring your own model

Works with OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and more. Use a powerful model for complex tasks and a lighter one for routine work. You pick what fits your budget.
